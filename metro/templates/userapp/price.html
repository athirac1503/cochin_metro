Kochi Metro – Fare Chart (Ticket Prices)

Kochi Metro Rail Ltd. (KMRL) fixed Kochi Metro Line-1’s fares in November 2016 with a minimum fare of Rs. 10 and maximum of Rs 60. Fare zones have been named F1, F2…F6, for distances ranging from 2 km (F1) from the point of origin; 5 km (F2); 10 km (F3); and increase in multiples of 5 km leading up to F6 (25 km from the point of origin). The fare increases by Rs. 10 for every additional zone.
